Three Acres Farm is set in a lovely river valley location – “our little piece of heaven”.  The thatched farm house is straight off the front of a chocolate box and is set in cottage gardens surrounded by about three acres of formal gardens, which includes three large fishing lakes.
Three Acres Farm is located in the hamlet of New Mills on the B3275, which is easily accessed off the A30.  In the adjacent village of Ladock, (about one mile distant), the Falmouth Arms, public house serves a good range of food and beverages and the chef has won awards from St Austell breweries for his desserts,  Ladock also has the nearest post office shop. 
The lovely city of Truro with its shops, restaurants and cathedral is only eight miles distant.


The coastal town and port of Falmouth is only 18 miles away to the southwest and Padstow with its’ famous Rick Stein connections is 17 miles to the northeast

Newquay with its’ surfing beaches is 10 miles away and Newquay airport with its regular flights in from Stansted and Gatwick is close by at St.Mawgan.  Newquay airport also offers the opportunity to fly out to the Isles of Scilly with the possibility to visit Tresco Gardens little bit of paradise.

Many gardens are within easy reach of Three Acres Farm by car including the famouse Eden Project and the Lost Gardens of Heligan, plus many National Trust properties, for example Trelissick, Glendurgan, Trerice and Lanhydrock.  A personal favourite garden to visit is the beautiful Trebah gardens, nr Falmouth.

In conclusion a warm welcome awaits you at Three Acres Farm, which is located in an ideal central location in the midst of all the holiday attractions Cornwall has to offer.
